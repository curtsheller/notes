#### Sequence

- C E F D (Interval: **1 3 4 2**)

```
C E F D | D F G E | E G A G | F A B C | G B C A | A C D B ||
```
Starts on the note is ended on. Stay in position, no need to do entire octave.

Easier to play using descending.
