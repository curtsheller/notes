## Fingering: Two Notes/Fingers Each Single String

- **1 2 3**
- 1 3 2
- 2 3 1
- 2 1 3
- 3 2 1
- 3 1 2

### Example

- Fingers **One**, **Two**, **Three**, fret (1), (2), (3)
- String Order: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1

This is like going up and down a ladder with three legs.

```
|-1-2-3---------------------------------------------------1-2-3-|
|------1-2-3-----------------------------------------1-2-3------|
|-----------1-2-3-------------------------------1-2-3-----------|
|----------------1-2-3---------------------1-2-3----------------|
|-----------------1-2-3---------------1-2-3---------------------|
|----------------------1-2-3-----1-2-3--------------------------|
|---------------------------1-2-3-------------------------------|
```

- Play notes right behind nthe frets, but not right on them.
- Clean sounding notes, no buzz. Let note right until next note.
